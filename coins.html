Coin City's journey began on Whatnot, where our passion for coins, precious metals, and collectibles quickly grew into a thriving live auction community. Featured Collection

Geiger Edelmetalle

Built for Security

Geiger bars are known for world-class anti-counterfeiting features. Their bars feature reeded edges and a proprietary, invisible varnish on the reverse side. When exposed to UV light, an illuminated LEV rhombus and the minting year become visible.

These advanced security features, combined with exceptional craftsmanship and elegant designs, have made Geiger products favorites among both investors and collectors worldwide.
